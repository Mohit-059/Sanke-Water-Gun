About Game

Rock-Paper-Scissors

Rock-Paper-Scissors is a classic hand game played between two people. It's a simple yet entertaining game that can be played anywhere, anytime.

Rules:

  • Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand:
    • Rock (a fist)
    • Paper (a flat hand)
    • Scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V)
  • The winner is determined by the rules:
    • Rock crushes Scissors (Rock wins)
    • Scissors cuts Paper (Scissors wins)
    • Paper covers Rock (Paper wins)
    • If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie.

Features:

  • Play against the computer in a fun and interactive way.
  • Easy-to-use interface with clear instructions.
  • Track your wins, losses, and draws to monitor your progress.
