About Game

Snake-Water-Gun

Snake-Water-Gun is a variant of Rock-Paper-Scissors, with each element representing different entities in a playful way.

Rules:

  • Each player simultaneously chooses one of three elements:
    • Snake (a snake motion with the hand)
    • Water (a pouring motion with the hand)
    • Gun (a shooting motion with the hand)
  • The outcome is determined by the following rules:
    • Snake drinks Water (Snake wins)
    • Water drowns Gun (Water wins)
    • Gun shoots Snake (Gun wins)
    • If both players choose the same element, it's a tie.

Features:

  • Engage in a lively game against the computer.
  • Intuitive interface with straightforward guidelines.
  • Track your victories, defeats, and ties to monitor your performance.
